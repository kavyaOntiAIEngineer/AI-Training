# Transformer Architecture & AI Application Architecture

A comprehensive study of the evolution of Transformer models, their internal architecture, attention mechanisms, contextual embeddings, and modern AI application pipelines. This repository contains presentation materials and learning resources that explain how state-of-the-art Large Language Models (LLMs) such as GPT and BERT work, along with the architecture used to deploy AI systems in production.

##  Key Takeaway

Transformers revolutionized Natural Language Processing by replacing sequential computation with self-attention, enabling highly parallel training, better contextual understanding, and the foundation for today's Large Language Models (LLMs). Combined with Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG), orchestration, safety mechanisms, and observability, they form the backbone of modern production AI applications.
